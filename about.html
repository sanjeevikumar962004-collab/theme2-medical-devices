Our Story

From a garage to global hospitals

2001

Founded in Boston

Stackly is founded by Dr. Sarah Chen with a $400K seed grant and a team of 6 biomedical engineers, operating out of a university lab space.

2006

First FDA Clearance

Our flagship patient monitoring system receives its first FDA 510(k) clearance, opening the door to US hospital procurement contracts.

2011

International Expansion

Stackly opens regional offices in London, Singapore, and Dubai, bringing our devices to 22 new countries within 18 months.

2018

AI-Powered Diagnostics

We launch our proprietary MediCore AI engine — the industry's first FDA-approved AI-assisted imaging analysis system integrated directly into radiology hardware.

2024

10,000 Hospitals Milestone

Stackly devices are now active across 10,000+ hospitals in 68 countries, processing more than 2 million scans daily.
